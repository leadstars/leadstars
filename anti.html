Welcome To Anti HIV AIDS Club

Leadstar Academy Anti HIV AIDS club is a student-run organization dedicated to raising awareness about HIV/AIDS and promoting safe sex practices among young people.


The club is committed to educating its members and the wider school community about the disease, its prevention, and the importance of early testing and treatment.

Through various activities such as seminars, workshops, and outreach programs, the club aims to reduce the stigma associated with HIV/AIDS and provide a supportive environment for those affected by the disease.


Members of the club also engage in fundraising activities to support HIV/AIDS research and advocacy efforts.


The club is open to all students who are interested in learning about HIV/AIDS and making a positive impact in their community.

By joining the club, students have the opportunity to develop leadership skills, gain valuable knowledge about public health, and contribute to a worthy cause.


Overall, Leadstar Academy Anti HIV AIDS club is an important organization that plays a vital role in promoting awareness and prevention of HIV/AIDS in the school community.


Its efforts have the potential to make a significant impact in reducing the spread of the disease and improving the lives of those affected by it.

The club is open to all students who are interested in learning about HIV/AIDS and making a positive impact in their community.
